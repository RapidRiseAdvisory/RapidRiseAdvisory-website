Engagement Parameters

What stage companies do you work with?

I focus primarily on Technology Readiness Levels, TRL 7 to 9. These ventures have moved beyond the lab stage and are demonstrating pilot capability or early commercial deployment. They are ready for institutional scaling, structured commercial contracts, and serious capital formation.

How does this differ from standard Venture Capital advisory?

Traditional software VC models rarely fit infrastructure and industrial scale ventures. I focus on the full capital stack required for physical industries, equity, debt, and grant funding, alongside the long term commercial contracts and counterparties needed to unlock that capital. The goal is bankability, not just fundraising.

Do you work with pure software companies?

Generally no. Pure SaaS and AI ventures are handled through our sister brand, RapidRise.io. RapidRise Advisory is focused on ventures with a physical, industrial, or sovereign component, such as energy, hardware, defence, critical infrastructure, or strategic supply chains.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Most engagements begin with a short diagnostic, then a focused work plan covering commercial contracts, capital strategy, and stakeholder mapping. I work hands on with founders and leadership teams to progress bankable outcomes, not just presentations.

What outcomes do clients typically achieve?

Clarity on the capital plan and funding pathway, improved credibility with institutional capital providers, stronger commercial contracting strategy, and faster alignment with strategic partners and counterparties required for long term scale.
