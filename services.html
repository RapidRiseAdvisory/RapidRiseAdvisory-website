Commercial Structure for Critical Industries.

Capital Readiness & Project Finance

Infrastructure and energy projects cannot scale on venture capital alone. I prepare technical ventures for institutional capital markets. This involves structuring the business case to meet the risk mandates of infrastructure funds, asset managers, and project finance lenders.

Focus: Bankability, Debt/Equity structuring, Investment Memorandum preparation.

Sovereign & Defense Procurement

For drone, maritime, and dual-use technology firms, the government is often the ultimate client. I guide companies through the acquisition channels of UK and European defense sectors. We frame technology as strategic sovereign capability rather than just a product.

Focus: Defense contracts, Government stakeholder mapping, Tendering strategy.

Industrial Offtake & Partnerships

Securing debt often requires secured revenue. I help negotiate and structure Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs), Joint Ventures, and long-term offtake agreements. These validate commercial demand before steel goes into the ground.

Focus: Commercial negotiation, Strategic Joint Ventures (JV), Supply chain integration.

Frequently Asked Questions regarding Commercial Advisory

How does RapidRise Advisory differ from a Placement Agent?

We are not a broker-dealer or a placement agent. We do not simply "shop" your pitch deck to investors. We are Commercial Architects. We work with your executive team to restructure your business model, contracts, and technical roadmap so that when you do approach institutional capital, your project is deemed "Bankable" rather than "Venture Risk".

At what TRL (Technology Readiness Level) do you engage?

We specialise in the TRL 7 to 9 transition. This is often called the "Valley of Death", where technology works in the lab but has not yet achieved commercial scale. If you are at TRL 1-4 (R&D focus), we are likely too late-stage for you. If you are operational but cannot scale revenue, we are the perfect fit.

Do you work on a Success Fee basis?

We work primarily on a retained advisory basis. Building Sovereign Resilience and Critical Infrastructure requires deep strategic work that cannot be aligned solely with a transaction fee. We partner with founders who are serious about long-term commercial structuring, not just a quick capital injection.

What sectors fall under "Hard Tech"?

We focus on capital-intensive industries that require complex physical deployment. This includes Marine & Renewable Energy, Defence & Dual-Use Technology, Semiconductor Supply Chain, and Advanced Manufacturing. We generally do not take on pure software (SaaS) clients on the Advisory side.

Can you help us secure a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) or Government Contract?

Yes. This is often the primary barrier to funding. We assist in structuring offtake agreements and government tenders to prove revenue certainty to your future investors.
